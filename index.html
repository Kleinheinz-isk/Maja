About Harry Styles

Harry Styles is a renowned British singer, songwriter, and actor. Born on February 1, 1994, in Redditch, Worcestershire, he gained fame as a member of the popular boy band One Direction.

After the band's hiatus, Harry pursued a successful solo career, releasing hit albums such as "Harry Styles" and "Fine Line." He is known for his distinctive voice, fashion sense, and charismatic stage presence.

Career Highlights

Harry Styles' career highlights include:

  • Formation and success with One Direction (2010-2015)
  • Solo debut with the self-titled album "Harry Styles" (2017)
  • Critical acclaim and commercial success with "Fine Line" (2019)
  • Acting roles in films like "Dunkirk" (2017) and "Don't Worry Darling" (upcoming)

Discography
